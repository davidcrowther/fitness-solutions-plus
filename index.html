FAQ’s

Q. Why have my previous exercise programs not worked?
Most programs are not tailor-made for your unique body; they are commonly cookie-cutter solutions that never work for everybody. How can a 35-year-old woman who wants to lose weight hope to get results from the same program that a 65-year-old man uses to improve his golf score? Other programs often have an over-reliance on training at the expense of nutrition or quality rest too.
Q. What’s the main key to getting results?
Personalizing a fitness program around your own body and your own goals; and then maintaining the consistency that comes from enjoying what you are doing and seeing the great results.
